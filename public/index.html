Welcome to the back-end of The PhantomRealm

    Routes are simple and easy to use.

    Routes Address Explaination
    User Route /users There is only one user which is the admin,
    customers don't need to register or login to see and purchase products.
    But you can add, register, login, delete and update users from the back-end if need be
    Products Route /products Gets all the products
    /products/:id Gets a specific product by it's id. You can also delete, add and update products
